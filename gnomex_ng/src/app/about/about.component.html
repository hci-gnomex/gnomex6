A LIMS for making and tracking requests for Next Generation Sequencing and genomic analysis

Import of externally generated genomic datasets in their native formats

Entry and association of structured annotations for the samples and experimental parameters with the data

Web tools to browse, search, modify, and download data files associated with the aforementioned data

GNomEx was developed by Huntsman Cancer Institute Research Informatics and designed in conjunction with the High Throughput Genomics Core Facility and the University of Utah Bioinformatics Core
